About me

My full name is Arnar Logi Hákonarson, but I usually go by the name of Arnar. (Or Jesus, if you are one of those people that think having a long hair and a beard is hilariously related to being called Jesus...) I am currently living in Copenhagen with my girlfriend Sylvia and I am studying Multimedia Design and communication at KEA as well as working part time at 66 North.

My main interests are music production and graphic design. If you are interested you can listen to some of my music Here


Origin

I am born and raised in a small fishing town in the western part of Iceland called Þingeyri, with the population of approx. 300 people. The town is surrounded with tall mountains and it takes about 45 minutes just to get to the next grocery store. Growing up we were only 3 in my class (including me).
